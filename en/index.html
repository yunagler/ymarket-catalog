FAQ

Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before getting started

What is the minimum order?
The minimum order is ₪1,600 + VAT. You can combine products from all categories in a single order.
What are the delivery times?
Delivery within 24-72 business hours to the Gush Dan and central region. For peripheral areas, up to 5 business days.
Are there payment terms?
Yes. For regular customers we offer payment terms of net + 30 up to net + 60, depending on the volume of activity.
How do I join the ordering portal?
Register for free through the website. Once approved, you'll get access to a tailored price list, 24/7 online ordering and order history.
Do you deliver nationwide?
Yes, we deliver all across Israel. Most activity is in Gush Dan and the center with fast supply, and we make regular deliveries to Haifa, Jerusalem, Beersheba, Ashdod and Eilat as well.
Can I get a quote?
Of course! Send us a product list via WhatsApp or by phone and we'll get back to you with a tailored quote within a few hours.
